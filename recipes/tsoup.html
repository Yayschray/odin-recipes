Creamy Tortellini Soup

Description

Tessa's favorite dish for any mealtime. An Itailian staple for family dinners; the tortellini, garlic and parmesan make for a hardy meal.

Perfect for refueling after a hard day's work, or just for forgetting in the back of your Honda Civic. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Shredded Carrots
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Chicken Broth
  • Frozen Cheese Tortellini
  • Baby Spinach
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
