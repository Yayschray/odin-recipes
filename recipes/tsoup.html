Creamy Tortellini Soup

Description

Tessa's favorite dish for any mealtime. An Itailian staple for family dinners; the tortellini, garlic and parmesan make for a hardy meal.

Perfect for refueling after a hard day's work, or just for forgetting in the back of your Honda Civic. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 Tblsp Butter
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tbls Italian Seasoning
  • Black Pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 Onion (diced)
  • 2/3 Cup Shredded Carrots
  • 1/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 6 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 16 oz. Frozen Cheese Tortellini
  • 1 Bag Baby Spinach
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in meduim saucepan
  2. Add garlic, itailian Seasoning, salt and pepper. Stir until garlic is cooked.
  3. Add onions and carrots, cook until tender.
  4. Slowly add flour, whisk constantly until sauce is smooth.
  5. Continue stirring and add broth. Bring to boil.
  6. Add spinach and tortellini to boiling mixutre, continue boiling until tortellini is cooked.
  7. Reduce heat to low, add cream and cook for additional 2 minutes.
  8. Add parmesan, stir and take off heat.
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
